Fun Trips for the whole family!

Discover two contrasting experiences in one day with our rafting trip on the Andrea and Tuti rivers! Start your journey with the serenity of the Andrea River, floating gently between lush landscapes and majestic mountains. Then, get your adrenaline pumping on the Tuti River, facing exciting and challenging rapids. Our expert guides will accompany you on these contrasting adventures, ensuring a day full of excitement and fun. Join us for an unforgettable experience of double thrills on the Andrea and Tuti Rivers!

What to Expect on this Trips:

  • Top quality rafting equipment, including paddles, life jackets and safety helmets.
  • Expert and certified guides who will accompany you throughout the trip, providing instructions and ensuring your safety on the water.
  • Admire the natural beauty of the surroundings, from lush rainforests to towering rocky canyons, as you make your way through the changing river landscape.
  • Experience moments of pure excitement and fun as you plunge through waves and dodge rocks, enjoying a rafting experience you'll never forget.
  • Refreshments and lunch to replenish your energy after an exciting day on the rivers.
  • Professional photographs to capture the most memorable moments with your family on the river.

Noah's Story!

In the far reaches of reality, daring pioneer Noah, in his 60s, ventured on an epic journey through the mysterious Paco, Alexa, Noah, Andrea and Tuti rivers. With indomitable courage and unmatched determination, he navigated tumultuous and serene waters, uncovering the deepest secrets and most challenging dangers. Driven by a passion to share these wonders with the world, Noah decided to create a website where anyone could access these enchanted rivers, guided by experts in the field.
