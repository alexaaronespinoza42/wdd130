Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to allow people from all over the world to have easy access to information about the company and its rafting services.

Audience

Our target audience is people between 18 and 50 years old who want to live the rafting experience with incredible views. The site should be aesthetically simple so that people quickly understand what is on it.
